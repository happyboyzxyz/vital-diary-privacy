Privacy Policy for Vital Diary

Effective Date: August 7, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect or share personal data. All diary entries are stored locally on your device using encrypted local storage (Hive database).

2. PIN Code and Access Protection

To protect your privacy, Vital Diary uses a PIN-based access system. You set your personal PIN when using the app. This PIN is stored securely on your device and is never transmitted or shared.

If you forget your PIN, we cannot recover it for you. You are responsible for remembering and safeguarding your PIN to maintain access to your records.

3. Third-Party Services

Vital Diary does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services.

4. Data Security

Your data stays on your device and is not uploaded or shared externally. We use local encryption mechanisms and PIN authentication to safeguard your personal entries.
